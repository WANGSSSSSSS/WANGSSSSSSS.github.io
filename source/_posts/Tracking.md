## What is object tracking?

Object tracking takes an initial set of detected objects, the process procedure contains identify the objects which lying in the initial set, then yields the movement of the given objects in each frame. In short, tracking task can be divided into identifying  targets and interpreting the trajectories of given target. The aim of this challenge is achieve optimal accuracy.
