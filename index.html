About

I am a PhD student at Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Limin Wang. My research centers on generative models—designing efficient and scalable architectures for image, video, and multimodal generation.

I build models that are simple, fast, and principled, from diffusion transformers and pixel-space generators to unified understanding-and-generation systems.
